Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Skystra ("we", "us", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Skystra mobile application, including during its beta testing period.

Information We Collect

Account information. When you create a Skystra account using an email address and password, we collect your email and a password hash (passwords are stored as a salted hash, not in plain text). If you sign in with Sign in with Apple, Apple shares with us a unique identifier and either your real email or an Apple-generated private relay email, based on your choice. You may also optionally share your name on first sign-in. We use this information solely to create and authenticate your account. You may optionally provide a display name, profile photo, and biographical details.

Content you create. Skystra stores the journal entries, posts, photos, and other content you create within the app. This content is associated with your account.

Location data. If you grant permission, Skystra accesses your device's location to attach geographic context to entries you create. Location is only collected when the app is in use and only when you actively use a feature that requests it.

Camera and photo library access. If you grant permission, Skystra can access your camera and photo library so you can capture and attach photos in the app. We only access photos you explicitly select or capture.

Push notification tokens. If you enable push notifications, your device generates a push token issued by Apple. We store this token, associated with your account, so we can deliver notifications to your device. The token does not by itself identify you to anyone other than us and Apple.

Usage and diagnostic data. We collect basic technical information such as crash reports and feature interactions to improve reliability. This data does not personally identify you.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Provide the core functionality of the app (storing your entries, syncing across your devices, displaying content)
  • Communicate with you about your account or important changes to the service
  • Diagnose and fix bugs, especially during the beta testing period
  • Improve the app's reliability and usability

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your content for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as described below.

Push Notifications

If you grant permission, Skystra sends push notifications for:

  • Daily sun events — local sunrise and sunset times based on the location associated with your account.
  • Social activity on your content — for example, when another user likes or comments on a post you have shared (this feature may be added during the beta period).

You can disable push notifications at any time in your device's iOS Settings under Notifications → Skystra, or within the app's settings. Disabling notifications does not delete your account or content.

How We Store Your Data

Skystra uses Supabase as its backend infrastructure provider. Your account data and content are stored in Supabase's databases and storage services, which are operated by Supabase Inc. Supabase acts as a data processor on our behalf. You can review Supabase's privacy practices at https://supabase.com/privacy.

Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and at rest using standard industry practices.

When you use map or location-search features within Skystra, those interactions are handled by Apple's MapKit service. Map tile requests and place searches are processed by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy and are not transmitted to our servers in personally identifying form.

Beta Testing Disclosure

Skystra is currently distributed via Apple's TestFlight as a beta product. Beta software may contain bugs that affect data handling. While we make reasonable efforts to protect your data, beta software is provided as-is.

Crash reports and diagnostic data collected by TestFlight are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights and Choices

You can:

  • Access your data by viewing it in the app.
  • Update or correct your information through the app's settings.
  • Delete your account and all associated data through account settings within the app, or by emailing wilkylegreer@gmail.com from the address associated with your account. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA, including the right to access, port, or restrict processing of your personal data. Email wilkylegreer@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

Children's Privacy

Skystra is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please email wilkylegreer@gmail.com and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the app or via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this policy will reflect the most recent revision.
